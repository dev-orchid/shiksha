## Core Modules

### 1. Student Management
- Complete student profiles with photos and documents
- Admission and enrollment tracking
- Student history and academic records
- Bulk import via CSV/Excel
- Parent/Guardian information linking
- Status tracking (Active, Inactive, Graduated, Transferred)

### 2. Staff & Teacher Management
- Comprehensive staff profiles
- Department and designation management
- Employment type tracking (Full-time, Part-time, Contract)
- Teacher-subject assignments
- Class teacher allocation
- Document management

### 3. Attendance Management
- **Student Attendance**
  - Daily attendance marking
  - Class-wise and section-wise tracking
  - Bulk attendance entry
  - Attendance reports and analytics
  - Late arrival tracking

- **Staff Attendance**
  - Clock-in/Clock-out system
  - Leave management
  - Attendance history
  - Monthly reports

### 4. Fee Management
- **Fee Structure**
  - Flexible fee categories (Tuition, Transport, Lab, etc.)
  - Class-wise fee configuration
  - Installment planning
  - Discount management

- **Fee Collection**
  - Invoice generation
  - Multiple payment modes (Cash, Online, Cheque)
  - Receipt printing
  - Payment tracking

- **Reports**
  - Collection reports
  - Outstanding dues
  - Class-wise collection
  - Date-range reports

### 5. Examination Management
- **Exam Configuration**
  - Multiple exam types (Unit Test, Mid-term, Final)
  - Flexible grading systems
  - Subject-wise scheduling

- **Results Management**
  - Mark entry
  - Grade calculation
  - Report card generation
  - Performance analytics

- **Grade Settings**
  - Customizable grade scales
  - Pass/Fail criteria
  - GPA calculation

### 6. Academic Configuration
- **Academic Years**
  - Session management
  - Year-wise data segregation

- **Classes & Sections**
  - Unlimited class creation
  - Multiple sections per class
  - Student capacity management

- **Subjects**
  - Subject master
  - Class-subject mapping
  - Subject types (Core, Elective)

### 7. Reports & Analytics
- Student reports
- Attendance analytics
- Fee collection summaries
- Academic performance trends
- Exportable to PDF/Excel

### 8. Communication (WhatsApp Integration)
- Bulk messaging
- Template management
- Group communication
- Message logs and tracking
- Automated notifications
